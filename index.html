Welcome to Mohammed's College

Founded in 1998, Mohammed's College has been at the forefront of providing quality education that combines academic rigor with practical skills development. Our institution is committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals who are prepared to make meaningful contributions to society.

World-Class Facilities

Explore our state-of-the-art campus resources

Modern Library

Our expansive library houses over 500,000 volumes, including rare manuscripts and extensive digital resources. The space features quiet study areas, collaborative workspaces, and 24/7 access during exam periods.

  • 500,000+ physical volumes
  • 24/7 access during exams
  • Digital resource center
  • Special collections archive
Take a Virtual Tour

Advanced Laboratories

Our cutting-edge research facilities include specialized labs for chemistry, biology, physics, and engineering. Students have access to industry-standard equipment and technology for hands-on learning.

  • 15 specialized labs
  • Industry-standard equipment
  • 3D printing and fabrication
  • Clean room facilities
Take a Virtual Tour

Athletic Complex

The college boasts a comprehensive sports facility including Olympic-size swimming pool, indoor basketball courts, fitness center, and outdoor fields. Our teams compete at national levels in multiple sports.

  • Olympic-size swimming pool
  • Indoor basketball courts
  • Fitness and wellness center
  • Outdoor athletic fields
